W00t 1st question on the q-bot! When can we expect another build? (Laocourn, 1 votes)
Abaddon We have established a build / hoc structure. Builds and HoC chats alternate each week. This week is a HoC chat, next week it will be a build. Week after that, it's HoC again, then a build. This system will progress for a long long time I imagine

 

With the new script system, will we be able to make NPCs unspawn after a certain amount of time or an event? (Laocourn, 1 votes)
Abaddon I imagine that will be distinctly possible, yes. We're providing you with a lot of hooks into the server, and allowing you to spawn things, delete things, adjust things, set up timers, and lots of other things as well. So it's distinctly possible that you will be able to do this, yes

 

Will you ever be implementing a young system for the emulator? similar or working as OSI's? (PoseidonXIX, 1 votes)
Abaddon Please people, read the previous HoC logs. They're available at www.uoxdev.com. This question specifically was answered last time. But to recap
Abaddon No, it's not likely. We've had basically no requests for this functionality at all, and we don't know how appropriate it may be. PR shard players tend not to be complete novices, and skill/stat gain is generally much faster on these shards.
Abaddon If there's a screaming desire for it, then we'll definitely have a look at a young system. But from my last request, absolutely no one emailed me requesting it.

 

In your next build will you fix the T2A walking bug? where you fall down cliffs and cant walk up? (PoseidonXIX, 1 votes)
Abaddon Well, I've scoured the boards today, and I haven't finished looking yet. There was a post about bugs in t2a, but there was almost no details at all, and not even sure if they were using 20c (You are using 20c, yes? :>)
Abaddon If people can email me with distinct descriptions, locations, and circumstances, then it can be looked into. But "the t2a walking bug" is almost meaningless in trying to track it down.
Abaddon My other thought is this: Last I checked, if you fell down a cliff, you couldn't walk back up it anyway (I mean, it is a cliff, and it's sheer :>)

 

When can we expect seeing the 'banaccount and 'addaccount commands to be functional? (PoseidonXIX, 1 votes)
Abaddon Well, one of the things that EviLDeD and myself have been working on is a much improved accounting system. The way it is currently implemented is poor, and it needs a complete work over, not a few patches, and that's being worked on by EviLDeD. It will likely come in with the new rearchitected version of UOX (same time as the Scripting engine)

 

Have you noticed PVP crashing issues? Last night on build 20c I was playing with some friends and whenever we'd get a hit on each other the server would crash. (Antitheus, 1 votes)
Abaddon No one has reported these issues to us. I haven't seen any posts on the board at all either. If you can reproduce it consistently, record a set of steps and information required to test it, and email it to me, Xuri, or submit it via the bug board
Abaddon We cannot fix bugs with little information. Verifying it on another setup would be a good thing to (making sure it's not script issues)

 

Is a change to townstones\factions in the works? (Antitheus, 1 votes)
Abaddon We are planning a number of changes to townstones, and regions, and a few other things. That has been worked on, but has been put on the backburner a touch, due to other things requiring a higher priority. But yes, there is a change in the works

 

How do you plan to compensate for OSI's super duper secret project, UO:3D? Talk of a completely new client and MULs, etc. In my eyes this could tear apart any and all emus dependent upon it's client and MULs. (Lump_The_No-Legged_Gamer, 1 votes)
Abaddon Well, the secret nature of it means it wouldn't be a simple thing to deal with. HOWEVER, I don't imagine it's particular insurmountable. I would be shocked if they redid both the client packet format and the mul files, because that would essentially mean redoing the client entirely. It may be what they're doing, but it would have a big effect on the server too.
Abaddon It'll be dealt with, there will be support for it long term, I can pretty much guarantee it. There's a lot of people who have a lot of interest in UO being emulated in the future

Abaddon Can't say for others, but for me, it's probably McDonalds, though Pizza Hut, Hungry Jacks, KFC and Red Rooster all come a close second

 

Currently there is a rather annoying bug in UOX causing Shopkeepers to have miss-named items for sale, e.g. the icon of a Chain Tunic with the description of a Pitchfork, one of the causes for this is adding items to a vendors pack in-game, any chance for a fix in the near future? (Zane, 1 votes)
Abaddon Well, from what I've gathered from others, this actually isn't entirely UOX's fault :> It's actually an OSI client issue. This does happen sometimes on OSI servers, and it happens to other emus as well. I imagine there are ways for us to minimize this situation, and we will look into it. But it's not entirely our fault :>

 

When will the UO 3D stuff be out and what all have they talked about to go along with it? (Cy, 1 votes)
Abaddon Well Cy, who knows when OSI will plan this?
Abaddon They haven't always been too crash hot at meeting deadlines, and I doubt they'll be particularly forthcoming about it until nearer the release.
Abaddon Suffice it to say, it will be supported in the long term.

 

How are things progressing with the new UOX improvements Abaddon and EviLDeD are working on? Any ETA? (Zane, 1 votes)
Abaddon Things are progressing nice and well on the improvements. I had a chance to work some more last night (been short of time recently). All known fixes from the last few builds have been integrated, and I embellished the script system some more with a few more implemented API calls
Abaddon I'm guessing that I now have over 100 working API calls, and a few supported events. Adding event support elsewhere should be fairly simple.
Abaddon I received Thyme's rewritten walking, it's incomplete and I haven't had a chance to look at it, but I should hopefully be able to take it from there (he has major RL commitments happening)
Abaddon So things are progressing nice and well, just short of time. Keeping up with posts on the board is hell :> And updating the documentation is stealing a lot of time too.

 

Any plans on enhancing stealing? (Antitheus, 1 votes)
Abaddon Long term yes, but it's not an immediate priority

Abaddon So yes, this has been looked into

Abaddon I've been trying to do my own bit of toolness, to tide things along, but it's hard to devote the time. I do have Decius' programs and source though, which I was going to hand over to the next tool team lead.

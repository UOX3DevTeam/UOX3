What Is UOX?

UOX stands for Ultima Offline Experiment and is an Ultima Online server emulator. The name is mostly historic, however a name change just for the beauty of it, is out of range.

With UOX you can:

  • Play with your UO client offline at home.
  • Play on your local LAN with your friends.
  • Serve on the internet with your own shard, where others can connect to.

It's Open Source (GPL), meaning that you can freely redistrebute and create your own derivates as long you also share your souce code.

UOX has a lot of features, is frequently updated & improved. It was one of the first (if not the very first) server emulators around, and in the years it has still influenced and inspired all other emu's in a major way.

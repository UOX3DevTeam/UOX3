        AREACOMMAND <command>

  Purpose: 
        Turns other commands into
        "area commands", performing
        their function over a
        specified area instead of just
        on a single item/character.
        Below follows a list of all
        commands that can be used
        along with this command:
		'areacommand dye ####
		'areacommand wipe
		'areacommand incx ####
		'areacommand incy ####
		'areacommand incz ####
		'areacommand newx ####
		'areacommand newy ####
		'areacommand newz ####
		'areacommand settype ####
		'areacommand newbie ####

        ADD 0x#### (HEX-ID from UO Client)
        ADD #### (Modelnumber from UO Client)
        ADD (To open GM Menu)

  Purpose: 
        Can be used to add "raw" items from
        either hex-id or modelnumber, or to
        open a GM Menu.
        Items added with this command will
        not have any special attributes or
        properties assigned.
